01 — Privacy Policy

Future Message does not collect any personally identifiable information. No account, email address, name, or phone number is ever required or requested.

The only data that exists is what you voluntarily create inside the app — messages, voice notes, and video notes — and that data never leaves your device.

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are embedded in the app.
  • No crash-reporting tools that transmit device data.
  • No advertising networks or third-party data brokers.
  • App Store purchase receipts are handled entirely by Apple; we receive no payment details.

All messages, voice memos, and video clips are stored in your device's local file system using iOS sandboxed storage. This means:

  • Data is accessible only to the Future Message app.
  • No other app on your device can read your messages.
  • If you use iCloud Backup, your messages may be included in that backup — governed by Apple's own privacy policy.
  • Deleting the app permanently erases all associated data from your device.

We strongly recommend keeping the app installed until all scheduled messages have been opened.

The app makes outbound network requests only in these specific cases:

  • App Store receipts — to validate Premium purchases via Apple's servers.
  • Push notification tokens — Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) delivery only; no message content is ever transmitted.

We do not use Google Firebase, Meta SDKs, or any other third-party data services. The app has no backend server of its own.

Notifications are scheduled locally on your device — no server is contacted. When a message unlock date arrives, iOS itself triggers the notification. Your message content is never sent anywhere to make this happen.

You may disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings without affecting your stored messages.

Future Message is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no personal data from anyone, there is no special data handling required for younger users. The app carries a 4+ age rating on the App Store and contains no age-gated content.

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where possible, notify users via an in-app banner on the next app launch.

Questions or concerns? Reach us at privacy@futuremessage.app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
